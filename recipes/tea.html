Cup of Tea

It's a cup of tea! From my home region of the Southern US to the lofty empire of British yesteryear, tea has been enjoyed by a variety of people around the world. It may seem simply to make, but let me tell you what! People are stupid and need guidance for all sorts of things!

Ingredients and Tools

  • Tea (for this recipe, *bagged*)
  • Water
  • Electric Kettle
  • Mug
  • Sugar (*optional)
  • Milk/cream (*optional)

Steps

  1. First, bring the water to boil in the electric kettle.
  2. While the water is boiling, place the tea bag in the mug with the string/label outside of the cup.
  3. Steep the bag in the water for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Remove the bag from the water.
  5. If you so desire, mix in sugar and milk to taste.

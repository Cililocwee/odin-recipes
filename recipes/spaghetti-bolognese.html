Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti is a classic "lazy dish" that honestly takes a little more work than most people give it credit for. However, if you're careful and follow the recipe, you don't have to be an Emeril Lagazzi or a Gordon Ramsay to make good, down-home spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (dried)
  • Ground meat
  • Onions (diced)
  • Garlic (freshly minced)
  • Olive oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. First, take the slices of bread and put them on a plate or cutting board.
  2. Next, if you will use mayo or mustard on your sandwich, spread the condiment in a thin later (to taste) on the slives of bread.
  3. After that, place slices of ham onto the open-faced sandwich (to taste).
  4. Finally, unite the two sides of the sandwich into on completed sandwich.
  5. Optional: Slice the sandwich from one corner to the opposite corner to make sandwich triangles, the superior way to consume a sandwich.
